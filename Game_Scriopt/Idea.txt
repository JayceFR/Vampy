Game Idea
The player is a vampire. His main aim is to infect as many people as he can. He can only walk around in the night with 
his own vamp vision. During the day 
The player is a human. During the night he is being haunted by a group of vampires. He can only kill the vampires with 
the shades of the moon which he gets in the day, when he kills the vampire they drop moon flowers. In the night, 
the vampires haunt the human by shooting their green blood. The game changes from Day to Night.
During the day, the player should collect the moon flowers which are dropped by the vampire.
